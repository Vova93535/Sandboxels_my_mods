# Hotter & Cooler Mod

This mod adds four powerful temperature control devices to Sandboxels.

## Elements Added

- **MEGA HOTTER** – heats all pixels within a 10‑pixel radius by +50°C every tick.
- **MEGA COOLER** – cools all pixels within a 10‑pixel radius by -50°C every tick (cannot go below -273°C).
- **999K HOTTER** – instantly sets the temperature of all pixels within a 3‑pixel radius to 999,999°C.
- **999K COOLER** – instantly sets the temperature of all pixels within a 3‑pixel radius to -999,999°C.

All devices are solid, insulated (they do not change their own temperature), and belong to the "machines" category.

## Installation

1. Open Sandboxels in your browser.
2. Click the **Mods** button on the toolbar.
3. Enter the full filename `hotcool_mod.js` and press Enter.
4. Refresh the page.

Alternatively, you can use the raw URL of this file:

    https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Vova93535/Sandboxels_my_mods/main/mods/Hotter_Cooler/hotcool_mod.js

Paste that URL into the mod manager instead of the filename.

## Usage Examples

- Place **MEGA HOTTER** next to water – it will boil and turn into steam within seconds.
- Place **MEGA COOLER** near lava – it will solidify into obsidian almost instantly.
- Use **999K HOTTER** to instantly vaporise anything in a small area.
- Use **999K COOLER** to freeze even the most resistant materials to near‑absolute zero.
